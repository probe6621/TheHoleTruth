Usage Guidelines

✓ DO

  • Use dark backgrounds with gold/cream text
  • Include the ∄0 axiom prominently
  • Maintain the elegant, ancient aesthetic
  • Use vignette/lighting effects for depth
  • Let the content breathe with spacing
  • Use geometric elements (circles, torus hints)

✗ DON'T

  • Use bright, neon colors
  • Make it look "sci-fi" or futuristic
  • Clutter with too many elements
  • Use casual or comic fonts
  • Add unnecessary decorative elements
  • Compromise readability for style

Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

  • Authoritative but accessible: Complex ideas made understandable
  • Ancient wisdom meets modern science: Bridge between eras
  • Reverent but not religious: Respect for the profound without dogma
  • Generous: Freely given, no gatekeeping
