An astrophysicist was the first to decode it. The mathematical precision is extraordinary.

Opposite Stonehenge, Wiltshire • July 7, 1996

One of the most famous formations ever: 915 feet long, containing 151 individual circles arranged in a perfect mathematical fractal pattern. Eyewitnesses reported it formed in under 45 minutes in broad daylight, opposite one of the busiest tourist sites in England.

The Julia Set is a fractal—a pattern that repeats at every scale, generated by iterating number equations infinitely.
